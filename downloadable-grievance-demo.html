Workflow Pipeline

Operational stages mirrored from the design document.

Stage 1 · Ingestion & Pre-Processing

  • Simulated WhatsApp intake
  • Message normalization
  • Language tagging
  • Translation to English

Stage 2 · Relevance Filter

  • Actionable complaint detection
  • Praise / greeting / spam filtering
  • Human review for ambiguity

Stage 3 · Location Resolution

  • District / taluk / ward extraction
  • Constituency mapping
  • Urban / rural governance tagging

Stage 4 · Department Mapping

  • Department classification
  • Category and issue mapping
  • Severity tagging

Stage 5 · Routing Decision

  • Legislative route
  • Executive route
  • Dual route
  • CM Office human review

Stage 6-7 · Dispatch, Tracking & Closure

  • Authority brief generation
  • Citizen acknowledgement
  • SLA monitoring and escalation
  • Closure and dispute handling
